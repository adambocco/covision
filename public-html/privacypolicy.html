    Covision Privacy Policy

    Overview

    This privacy policy will explain how Covision uses the personal data we collect from you when you use the website.

    Last updated: December 7, 2020

    What data is being collected?

    Personally Identifiable Information:

    • First Name
    • Last Name
    • E-Mail Address

    How will we use your data?

    Covision collects your data so that you can:

    • Login
    • Manage Your Donations
    • Post Your Facilities
    • Modify Your Facilities
    • Request PPE
    • Donate PPE

    How do we store your data?

    Covision securely stores your data using Google Cloud Platform servers.

    We will keep your email as long as you use the service. If you choose to terminate your account, your data will be securely erased.

    Marketing

    No advertising agencies are affiliated with this site, nor is the information given to any of them.

    What are your data protection rights?

    Covision would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

    Right of access – You have the right to request Covision for copies of your personal data.

    Right to rectification – You have the right to request that Covision correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Covision to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

    Right to erasure – You have the right to request that Covision erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

    Right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Covision restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

    Right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Covision’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

    Right to data portability – You have the right to request that Covision transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

    What are Cookies?

    Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology. For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

    How do we use cookies?

    Covision uses cookies to improve your experience on our website, which includes:

    • Keeping you logged in

    What types of cookies do we use?

    Functionality – Covision usescookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.

    How to manage cookies

    You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

    Privacy policies of other websites

    Covision links to other websites. Our privacy policy only applies to our website. If our site were to link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

    Changes to our privacy policy

    Covision keeps their privacy policy page under regular review. The last time the page is updated will be found on the overview section.

    How to contact us

    If you have any questions about Covision’s privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.
